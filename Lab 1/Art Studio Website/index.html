Welcome to Pixel Art Studios!
Our studio invites you to open your atristic side and relieve any stress through artistic expression. We offer a wide range of classes; from traditional watercolor art to 3D designs. Our instructors are skilled and helpgul. They are happy to work with new artists as well as with those who seek to improve their skill. Take a look at our classes and find what interests you the most.
